Landmarks On Muntinlupa

It is classified as a highly urbanized city with a population of more than 400,000 and is given the nickname "Emerald City of the Philippines" by the tourism establishment. Muntinlupa is a home to some of the best commercial establishments in the metropolis and is the location of Ayala Alabang Village, one of the country's biggest and most expensive residential communities, where many of the wealthy and famous live.

The following are the must-see places when you visit Muntinlupa City;


Jamboree Lake

Jamboree Lake is a small recreational lake in the Muntinlupa area of the Philippines. It's the country's tiniest natural lake, measuring 1.5 hectares and nestled amid the 587-hectare New Bilibid Prison Reservation. It is one of two lakes located entirely within the borders of Metro Manila, the other being the artificial La Mesa Dam and Reservoir in Quezon City.

See Jamboree Lake


Memorial Hill

The burial site of Major Eriberto B. Misa Sr., located a few meters from the lakefront, served as a monument to the former Bureau of Jail Director for his 12 years of service, making him the longest-serving prison director from 1937 to 1949. This hill in Muntinlupa City's New Bilibid Prison Reservation houses a vintage Japanese cannon from World War II and a grotto statue of Our Lady of Lourdes where regular masses were conducted before the foundation and installation of the Our Lady of Mercy Chaplaincy in the early 1950s. The Memorial Hill is just one of many examples of why young people should be taught not to vandalize and respect historical sites.

See Memorial Hill


Sports Complex

Sports Complex is a Stadium, Premise located at Tunasan Baywalk Buendia St. Tunasan, Muntinlupa, Metro Manila Philippines. You can see here the Laguna bay, the Oval field and Olympic pool.

See Sports Complex


Museo ng Muntinlupa

The Muntinlupa Museum, commonly known as the Muntinlupa City Museum, is located on Centennial Avenue in Tunasan, Muntinlupa, Metro Manila, Philippines. A main gallery, a mayor's hall, an interactive science center, and a theater with a seating capacity of 200 persons are all located within the Museum. It has displays spanning the city's history from precolonial times to the present day. It also hosts temporary exhibitions and works by current artists from the city.

See Museo ng Muntinlupa


Festival Mall

Festival Alabang, formerly known as Festival Supermall, is a shopping mall owned and operated by Filinvest Land, Inc.'s subsidiary, Filinvest Alabang, Inc. Filinvest City (previously Filinvest Corporate City) is a shopping mall in Alabang, Muntinlupa, Philippines. The mall first opened its doors on May 15, 1998. The mall is the Philippines' sixth largest mall, with a gross floor space of 400,000 square meters. It is one of the most prestigious retail centers in the South. In the first quarter of 2014, the administration finished the first phase of the River Park mall extension. In 2017, the Water Garden, a portion of the Expansion Wing, opened.

See Festival Mall


Central Park Filinvest Alabang

A breath of freshness at the center of the city, the Central Park will have a distinct, lush landscape for residents, workers and visitors to enjoy. Promenade St, Alabang, Muntinlupa, Metro Manila. Central Park is accessible and easy to locate. It is found in the Spectrum District of Filinvest City, Alabang, intersecting Spectrum Linear Park in Spectrum Midway, in between Commerce Avenue and Parkway Avenue in Alabang, Muntinlupa City. The park is also just two minutes away from Festival Mall and three-minutes away from Filinvest Tollgate going to South Luzon Expressway, making the area accessible and easy to locate.

See Central Park Filinvest Alabang


Sunken Garden

The Muntinlupa Sunken Garden, also known as the New Bilibid Prison Sunken Garden and often shortened to simply Sunken Garden, is a large public urban park and a local government-protected zone in Muntinlupa. It is an open grassy space on the southern shore of Jamboree Lake that is lower than the surrounding elevation in the New Bilibid Prison. The Muntinlupa Sunken Garden is in the central area of the Poblacion village at the eastern section of the 587-hectare (1,450-acre) prison reservation. It is an open grassland delineated by large canopy trees that serves as a buffer between New Bilibid Prison and the communities in east Poblacion.

See Sunken Garden

Alabang Town Center

Alabang Town Center, or “Town” as it is affectionately referred to by its regulars, is the premier suburban lifestyle center south of Metro Manila. Standing right next to gated residential communities and bustling business developments, Alabang Town Center serves as the community’s second home offering a refuge to relax, converge and shop at one’s own pace. The Corte, the Town's Mediterranean-inspired courtyard, invites you to take leisurely walks while enjoying the seamless blend of natural elements with retail, dining, and entertainment. With its multiple al fresco areas, Alabang Town Center’s engaging spaces add to the homey ambiance that befits its community’s lifestyle.

See Alabang town center
